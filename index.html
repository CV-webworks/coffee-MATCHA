Our Story

At Coffee & MATCHA, we believe in the art of simplicity and the power of quality. Our café is a sanctuary where traditional Japanese matcha ceremonies meet the comfort of expertly crafted coffee.

Every cup is a journey. From ethically sourced beans to ceremonial-grade matcha, we're committed to delivering an experience that awakens your senses and nourishes your soul.
