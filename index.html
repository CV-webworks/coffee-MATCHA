Our Story

Crafted with
care and calm

Coffee & Matcha is a neighbourhood café in the heart of New Barnet — built for those who believe a great drink deserves more than a quick pour. We source our coffee with care and prepare ceremonial grade matcha with the same reverence.

Whether you're settling in for the morning, pausing between errands, or simply seeking a quiet moment — this is your space. No rush. No noise. Just exceptional drinks in a considered environment.
