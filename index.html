Our Story

Crafted with Intention

Every element of Coffee & MATCHA has been thoughtfully designed to create an experience that nourishes both body and soul.

Founded on the principles of mindfulness and quality, we've created a space where the ancient Japanese tea ceremony meets contemporary coffee culture. Each cup tells a story of heritage, craft, and dedication.

Our matcha is stone-ground from shade-grown tea leaves in Uji, Japan, maintaining the traditional methods passed down through generations. Our coffee beans are ethically sourced and expertly roasted to reveal their unique terroir and flavour profiles.

This isn't just a café—it's a ritual, a pause, a moment of presence in your day. Welcome to your new sanctuary.
